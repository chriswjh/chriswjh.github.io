Curriculum Vitae

Jenq-Haur Wang is a professor in Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering, National Taipei University of Technology (Taipei Tech), Taiwan. He received his BS and Ph.D. degrees from Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering, National Taiwan University (NTU), Taiwan, in 1994 and 2002, respectively. During Oct. 2002 and Feb. 2007, Dr. Wang conducted his postdoctoral research in Institute of Information Science, Academia Sinica, Taiwan, under the supervision of Dr. Lee-Feng Chien. He joined Taipei Tech as an assistant professor in 2007. His research interests include social Web mining, knowledge discovery, big data analytics, distributed machine learning, and network and information security.

More information can be found at the following pages for my short biography or some detailed description about me.
