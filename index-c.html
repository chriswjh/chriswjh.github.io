個人簡歷

Jenq-Haur Wang is a professor in Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering, National Taipei University of Technology (Taipei Tech, 國立台北科技大學), Taiwan. He received his BS and Ph.D. degrees from Department of Computer Science and Information Engineering, National Taiwan University (NTU), Taiwan, in 1994 and 2002, respectively. During Oct. 2002 and Feb. 2007, Dr. Wang conducted his postdoctoral research in Institute of Information Science, Academia Sinica, Taiwan, under the supervision of Dr. Lee-Feng Chien(簡立峰). He joined Taipei Tech as an assistant professor in 2007. His research interests include social Web mining, knowledge discovery, big data analytics, distributed machine learning, and network and information security. Since Jul. 2023, he also serves as the Deputy Director of Cybersecurity Research Center at Taipei Tech. In November 2024, he joined Joint Commission of Technological and Vocational College Admission Committee as the Deputy Chief Executive Officer.
