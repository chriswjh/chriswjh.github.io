Data Mining, Spring 2023

This course offers an introduction to the principles and concepts in data mining, which is gaining more popularity in recent years.
The course is offered for the Application Section of the UTA+NTUT Dual Degree Program. It's taught by two instructors: Jenq-Haur Wang, and Mohammad.
This page is for the first part. The major topics covered in the first part include: data mining and distributed processing.
