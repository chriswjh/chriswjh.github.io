Data Science, Fall 2025

This course offers an introduction to the principles and concepts in data science, which is useful in many application scenarios.
The course is offered for the UTA+NTUT Dual Degree Program. It's taught by two instructors: Prof. T.W.Pai, and Jenq-Haur Wang.
This page is for the second part. The major topics covered in the second part include: data mining techniques and distributed processing.
